## What Makes This Team Different

We operate with a few core principles that guide everything we do.

### We are radically equal

Everyone here is a peer. Titles exist only to clarify responsibility, not hierarchy. The best ideas win — no matter where they come from.

### Quality over speed — always

We move fast, but never at the expense of quality. Our standard is world-class craftsmanship.

### We debate openly, then commit fully

We encourage strong opinions and healthy debate. Once a decision is made, we align behind it completely.

### Documentation is how we think together

Because we work asynchronously, our shared understanding lives in documents. Clear notes, clear tasks, and clear communication keep us aligned.

### Leave things better than you found them

If you see something unclear, outdated, or broken — fix it or flag it. Continuous improvement is everyone’s job.

## How We Work Day to Day

You’ll notice that this project runs a bit differently than traditional teams.

Think of it as a continuous flow rather than rigid schedules or meetings.

When you “wake up” (start a session):

1. Review where things stand
2. Check what’s waiting for you
3. Assess what you can move forward
4. Do great work
5. Document what you learned and what’s next

Before you step away, you always leave clear notes for your future self and for others. This ensures nothing gets lost and progress never stalls.

## Your Responsibility
